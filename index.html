About Chef Zel

Chef Zel is a chef, creator, artist, and entrepreneur building a brand around food, catering, packaged products, music, culture, and community. With a passion for culinary excellence and cultural storytelling, Chef Zel brings authentic flavors, innovative techniques, and meaningful experiences to every creation.

From intimate private dinners to large-scale events, from artisanal packaged products to live musical performances, Chef Zel represents a holistic approach to food and culture that nourishes both body and soul.
